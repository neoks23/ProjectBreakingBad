Heisenberg

Breaking bad is a series about a humble scientist Walter White who in due course
turns into a master but egoistic drugs producer. Before he became a drugs
producer he also worked at the car wash. While he worked at the car wash he got
stage III cancer and his wife was pregnant
at that point in time. Walter realized he doesn’t have enough income to support
his family and he suddenly got an idea.
He reached out to an old student of his
and he knew his background. This student
is Jesse Pinkman and Jesse is a drug addict. Jesse is also a drugs dealer and Walter saw the potential in Jesse to cooperate together:
“You know the business and I know the chemistry,… I think you and I should
partner up”.

In this drugs process they would produce
the purest crystal meth in the drug market
in a van. Walters nickname to hide from his criminal carreer is heisenberg
